About Me

Hello! I’m Joe Brands.
From a very young age I knew I wanted to be a mechanical engineer and passion for aviation has shaped my career. From flying and working with aircraft to designing cutting-edge aviation products, I thrive in an environment that challenges me to innovate and improve. I am looking for impactful projects that I can be excited to pour my engineering passion into and a team that shares my enthusiasm.

I have a natural curiosity for understanding systems and processes, always looking for ways to make them better through efficiency and attention to detail. Coupled with a strong financial mindset, I enjoy finding engineering solutions that are effective but also economically viable.


I pride myself in being a power user of many software packages and quickly mastering software tools. This enables me to leverage technology to drive timely results and multiply my productivity.
